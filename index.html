Pierre Auger Observatory

I am an official member of the Pierre Auger Collaboration. I have been pursuing my PhD Thesis in the framework of the Pierre Auger Observatory. The Pierre Auger Observatory is devoted to the study of the most energetic particles known to mankind, the Ultra High Energy Cosmic Rays. I worked on the problem of the mass composition of UHECRs. My work involves creating computational physics models and performing simulations. I also perform data analysis.
